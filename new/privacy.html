Privacy Policy

Please read our policies carefully before using KwikLearn's website and services.

Last updated: March 2026
Home / Privacy Policy

KwikLearn respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, join our waitlist, contact us, or use our services.

1

Information We May Collect

We may collect basic information such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone or WhatsApp number if you contact us
  • Messages or inquiries you send to us
  • Basic website usage information such as browser type or general visit activity
2

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to your questions or inquiries
  • Provide updates about KwikLearn
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience
  • Communicate about partnerships, support, or early access opportunities
3

How We Protect Your Information

We aim to take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. However, no online method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4

Sharing of Information

KwikLearn does not sell your personal information. We may only share information when necessary to operate our services, comply with legal obligations, or protect our rights.

5

Children's Privacy

Because KwikLearn is education-focused, we take privacy seriously. Where needed, parents, guardians, or schools may be involved in supporting student access and use of our services.

6

Third-Party Services

Our website or future services may contain links to third-party platforms such as social media pages, Google Play, or other tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites or services.

7

Your Choices

You may contact us if you would like to ask about the information you have shared with us or request updates to your details.

8

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Have Questions?

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us.
